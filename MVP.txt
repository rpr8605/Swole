# User Story

As a fitness enthusiast I want to be able to get all my steps in each day in my busy life so that I can meet my fitness goals.


# Identify Target Audience

Anyone that is looking to keep track of fitness goals


# Identify Problems That Product Will Address

Give a additional set of motivation to reaching your daily Step goals

# Primary Goal of Product

Help people to achieve fitness goals.
